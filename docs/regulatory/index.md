# Regulatory

> *Reporting standards, regulatory pathways, and the documentation an auditor will ask for. The chapter that turns a published model into a deployable product.*

- **[CONSORT-AI and SPIRIT-AI](consort-spirit.md)** — the modern reporting standards for AI clinical trials and the protocols that lead to them.
- **[DECIDE-AI](decide-ai.md)** — the reporting standard for the early-stage clinical evaluation of decision-support systems.
- **[FDA pathways for AI/ML CDS](fda-pathways.md)** — Software-as-a-Medical-Device, the 21st-Century-Cures CDS carve-out, IDE/IND, the predetermined-change-control plan, and what an auditor will actually ask for.

The companion chapters in NeuroStack ([AI/ML → Regulatory & clinical deployment](https://phindagijimana.github.io/neuro_stack/ai/regulatory/)) cover the imaging-side regulatory framing; PrecisionStack focuses on the decision-support and trial side.
